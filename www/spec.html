Regos are open

Peter Shaw Posted on Friday, April 1st, 2016Friday, April 1st, 2016 by Peter Shaw

Registrations are open for our 2016 Winter touch football season. Located at Kensingtons Holland Park, Melbourne’s best touch football fields. We run on Wednesday nights and our season is due to commence on the 18th of May.

To learn more about the fantastic location, click here:

https://princesparktouch.com/about/winter-location/

And more info about our Winter competition is available here:
https://princesparktouch.com/about/

To enter a team or join as an individual follow the instructions below.

Team Entry

To enter a team please download and complete the following Team Registration Form
and email it to: info@princesparktouch.com.

If you have not collected all your players details do not worry (we will send you a link where you can add additional names later), just ensure the following are details are recorded:

  • Team name
  • Captains details (phone number and email)
  • Approximate standard of team. (your grade will be determined over the first two weeks which are grading rounds)

Once we have received your form we will hold a place for you in the competition. The total cost per team will be $1290 for the season (all inclusive). deposit is $490 (this is required before the first game).

Individual players

PPT will try to place individual players into team right up to the commencement of the season. If you are an individual player wishing to be placed in an existing team visit Individual Players and follow the instructions.

Or for any other enquiries please contact us on:

    E-mail: info@princesparktouch.com
  • Brad Chant (Mob) 0400 070 077

PPT Winter preseason program

Peter Shaw Posted on Thursday, March 31st, 2016Thursday, March 31st, 2016 by Peter Shaw

In an effort to build the social touch community in the inner north of Melbourne, this year Princes Park Touch will be running the PPT pre season programme.

In May we will be providing team coaching for existing teams and an organised and refereed scratch match for unaligned players looking to start playing touch or those just keen to upgrade their skills or get back into it. This night will also be used to assess unaligned players and place them into teams to play in the PPT competition.

  • When: Wednesday the 11th of May at 6:30
  • Where: JJ Holland Park, Kensington. To view a map please click here..

If an existing team wants to take advantage of some coaching they are requested to let us know and we’ll ensure that we have an experienced coach in attendance specifically for their team. However informal coaching and formal refereed touch will be available for all those in attendance.

Male players who are available to play mens touch please contact me before the preseason night, I need to know earlier mens competition starts a week earlier

Any questions please contact:

  • Peter Shaw (Mob) 0414 315 957
    E-mail: info@princesparktouch.com

TFA Level 1 Referee Course

Peter Shaw Posted on Wednesday, March 30th, 2016Wednesday, March 30th, 2016 by Peter Shaw

Touch Football Victoria will be facilitating a Level 1 Referee Course on Sunday 17 April 2016 for participants interested in becoming a nationally accredited TFA Referee.

If you wish to refere with us then we will cover the the Course fee is $44.00 per participant inclusive of materials. Registration can be completed online via the following link – Level 1 Referee Course Registration.

Course: Level 1 Referee Course

Location: Harlequin Club, Power Avenue, Ashwood

Date: 17 April 2016

Fee: $44.00

Time: 10:00am – 2:00pm

For more information, please contact (03) 9861 3000 or visit Touch Football Victoria.
